About Me

I live in Longmont, Colorado. Being outdoors and experiencing the environment around me is at the top of my love list. But, more than anything, I live for traveling (domestically and abroad) and learning about new places and cultures! I also enjoy endurance sports and appreciate the mental and physical challenge of pushing my mind and body further than ever seems possible.

In the end, all my life passions inspire my design work. Enjoy reviewing part of my portfolio and reach out with any questions (morning, noon or night). I hope we can work together in the future to create compelling designs that cause consumers to act. Cheers!
